Password Generation Functionality

Users shall be able to generate secure passwords with length as a customizable parameter. The generated passwords shall meet industry-standard security guidelines.

Password Strength Assesment

The system shall assess the strength of passwords based on complexity, length, and other relevant factors. Users shall be provided feedback on the strength of their passwords.
